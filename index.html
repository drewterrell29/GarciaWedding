Our story together

What can I say? If our story was made into a movie, it would be a rather short one. Short and sweet. We both felt that if we were to date it should lead somewhere with the goal of marriage. We didn’t want to be drifting off into nowhere wondering what’s next. So with that in mind we wanted to be intentional and purposeful. The word love doesn’t even make sense without God, and our relationship could have never survived without our relationship with Christ. Annalynn and I are two broken people. Yes, we are broken. If we waited to be perfect before we started dating…well, you wouldn’t be reading this right now. The grace of God abounds in every step we take, so we don’t have to be perfect, Christ is. We have made our first relationship with Christ and that is our foundation!

Lui Garcia
